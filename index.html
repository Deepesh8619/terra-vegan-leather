Material Catalogue

Each material is engineered to meet or exceed the tensile strength, abrasion resistance, and colourfastness of conventional leather. Order a single material or mix across all six in one shipment.

01

Cactus Leather

Sourced from Oaxaca, Mexico — Desserto® certified

Derived from the mature pads of the nopal cactus, harvested in six-month rotations without uprooting the plant. Each pad is sun-cured for 72 hours, milled to a fine compound, and pressed into uniform sheets with a naturally water-repellent, matte grain. No synthetic sealants required. Suitable for die-cutting, embossing, and foil stamping.

Water-Repellent UV-Stable Breathable Embossable
Thickness 0.8 – 1.4 mm
Roll Width 1.37 m (54″)
MOQ 50 sqm
Lead Time 10 – 14 days
02

Mycelium Leather

Lab-cultivated in the Netherlands — Mylo™ process

Grown from the subsurface root networks of fungi in climate-controlled bioreactors over a 14-day cultivation cycle. The resulting mat is tanned using plant-based agents, yielding a dense, full-grain sheet with inherent flex memory. Thickness is tuneable at production — specify your gauge at order. Accepts screen-print, laser-etch, and hot-press finishing.

Biodegradable Custom Gauge Carbon-Negative Laser-Compatible
Thickness 0.6 – 2.0 mm
Roll Width 1.50 m (59″)
MOQ 100 sqm
Lead Time 18 – 21 days
03

Piñatex®

Milled in the Philippines — Ananas Anam licensed

Constructed from the long fibres of pineapple leaves — an agricultural residue that would otherwise be incinerated after harvest. The fibres are decorticated, felted into a non-woven mesh, and coated with a bio-resin top layer. The result is a lightweight, breathable textile with a distinctive woven grain that cannot be replicated synthetically. Each batch carries slight natural variation.

Lightweight Breathable Waste-Stream Material PETA-Approved
Thickness 1.0 – 1.2 mm
Roll Width 1.37 m (54″)
MOQ 50 sqm
Lead Time 12 – 16 days
04

Apple Leather

Processed in Bolzano, Italy — Frumat® technology

Manufactured from the pomace left behind by the Italian juice industry — skins, cores, and seeds that would otherwise enter landfill. The pomace is dried at low temperature, ground to a 200-micron powder, and bonded onto a cotton-poly backing with a water-based polyurethane matrix. The surface is unusually soft to the touch and resists common staining agents including coffee, wine, and ink.

Ultra-Soft Stain-Resistant Food-Waste Origin Cotton Backing
Thickness 0.7 – 1.2 mm
Roll Width 1.40 m (55″)
MOQ 50 sqm
Lead Time 14 – 18 days
05

Grape Leather

Produced in Milan, Italy — Vegea® patent

Derived from grape marc — the pressed skins, stalks, and seeds discarded annually by the Italian wine industry. The marc is dried, processed into a bio-oil, and combined with natural fibres to form a flexible, durable composite. The production process uses zero water beyond what is present in the raw marc itself. Available in a warm tonal range from burgundy to deep umber without added dye.

Zero-Water Process Flexible Natural Pigment Chemical-Free
Thickness 0.9 – 1.3 mm
Roll Width 1.37 m (54″)
MOQ 75 sqm
Lead Time 14 – 20 days
06

Cork Leather

Harvested in Alentejo, Portugal — FSC® certified oak forests

Stripped from the outer bark of cork oak trees in nine-year cycles — the tree is never felled, and each harvest stimulates new bark growth. The bark is boiled, flattened, and laminated onto a cotton or recycled polyester backing. The resulting sheet is inherently waterproof, fire-retardant to Class B1, and hypoallergenic. It weighs roughly half of equivalent-thickness bovine leather.

Waterproof Fire-Retardant B1 Hypoallergenic 50% Lighter
Thickness 0.5 – 1.8 mm
Roll Width 1.40 m (55″)
MOQ 50 sqm
Lead Time 7 – 12 days
