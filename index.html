FAQ

Questions
buyers ask us.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Most materials start at 50 sqm. Mycelium is 100 sqm, grape is 75 sqm. Sample kits are free.

Can I mix materials in one order?

Yes. Combine any of the six as long as each meets its individual MOQ. One invoice, one shipment.

Do you offer custom colours?

All materials at 150 sqm minimum per colour. Send a Pantone reference, we match within 2 delta-E.

Which countries do you ship to?

40+ countries via DHL, FedEx, sea freight. US, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Australia, Japan, UAE and more.

How does it compare to real leather?

Matches tensile strength (15–25 N/mm²), abrasion (50,000+ Martindale), colourfastness (Grade 4+). Lighter, no tanning chemicals, REACH compliant.

What certifications do you hold?

ISO 9001, PETA Approved Vegan, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, GRS, REACH compliant.

What is your quality guarantee?

Every roll ships with QC report. If specs aren't met, we replace at our cost or full refund.
